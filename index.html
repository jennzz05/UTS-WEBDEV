About North Sulawesi

Where Tradition Meets Adventure

From the world-class diving paradise of Bunaken and the serene Lake Tondano to the volcanic peaks of Lokon and Klabat, North Sulawesi offers breathtaking natural wonders. Experience vibrant cultural traditions like the Kabasaran dance, visit historic sites such as Ban Hin Kiong Temple, and enjoy bold Minahasan cuisine from rica-rica to mie cakalang.

Here, every journey blends nature, culture, and flavor—inviting you to discover the true spirit of Sulawesi Utara.
